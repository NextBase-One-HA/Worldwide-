Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 27, 2026

1. Service Overview

Our service, "Smile / Friend", provides a secure data processing environment. We facilitate the secure transmission of data between "Smile Base" (Input) and "Friend Base" (Output).

2. Data Processing and Security

Smile / Friend processes user-submitted text data only for the purpose of real-time transformation and analysis.

  • No Persistent Storage: Data is processed temporarily in volatile memory and is not stored on our servers after the session ends.
  • No AI Training: We do not use user data to train, improve, or fine-tune our AI models.
  • Encryption: All data in transit is protected via industry-standard SSL/TLS encryption.

3. Information We Collect

We do not collect personal identifiers or any sensitive data beyond what is necessary to perform the service requested by the user within the app.
